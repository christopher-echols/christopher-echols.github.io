About

An autobiography in eight sentences.

I love making things. And telling stories.

Growing up, I wanted to be an illustrator or a wildlife photographer. Instead, I ended up editing television shows and designing websites for a living.

For over 20 years now, I've proudly given my all for the University of Tennessee, where I am currently the Director of Digital Communications. I've also done work for ESPN, HGTV, A&E, The History Channel, and NASCAR, among others (although I wish National Geographic was on this list).

My wonderful wife and I have two utterly stupendous kids.

In my spare time, I draw birds.

This website is built the old fashioned way using nothing but HTML and CSS. Like any handmade thing, it is imperfect. Please let me know if you find something broken so I can repair it.
